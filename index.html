    Wholesome Breakfast

    1. Get goat's kefir and pour into bowl with raspberries and strawberries and blueberries
    2. Make an egg omlette style and fold it and cut it up
    3. Cut up half an avocado that looks fresh
    4. Pour olive oil onto eggs
    5. Put toast in toaster oven and then put a slice of cheddar cheese on it when it's done
